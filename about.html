I believe in devops, agile practices, open source and making code a social and humanistic endevour. I also believe that processes, tools and team interaction are as important as individual prowes, and that high performing teams are the main artifact to strive to. ### Me and software As with other things of the heart, it is difficult to explain my feelings for software develompent, but I'll try. Software development brings with it supremely grattifying experienses: the pride of defeating frustration, the thrill of finding the solution to a puzzle, the sense of accomplishment of constantly learning new things, the intellectual fullfilment of meeting intelligent and interesting people, and the camaraderie of achieving a goal with a team.
